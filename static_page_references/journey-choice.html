Sport Relief Giving Pages

Whether you're fundraising on your own or with others, set up a Sport Relief Giving Page to get sponsored and collect cash instantly and securely online.

How old are you?

How will you be fundraising and taking part?

I'm fundraising on my own A personal Giving Page is perfect for people fundraising on their own be it a sponsored silence or bike ride. You can personalise it with photos, send “please sponsor me” messages and turn it into an online bucket to collect the cash. It collects all the cash you raise and adds it automatically to your fundraising total

If you're doing a solo activity like a sponsored silence, this is where you start.

I'm fundraising with others A Team Giving Group Page should be set up if you are fundraising with other people if that’s with friends, family or on behalf of your School, Nursery or Youth Group. It enables a fundraising teams to raise money towards a collective team total. Each team member’s individual sponsorship will count towards the team total – and you’ll be able to be sponsored as a team, nursery, primary school, secondary school or youth group if you wish. By setting up a Team Giving Page you will become the Team Captain and be responsible for inviting other people to join your Team and personalising the Team Giving Page. Whenever anyone joins your team they will get their own personal Giving Page too.

If you want to fundraise with friends, family or on behalf of your School, Nursery or Youth Group, become a Team Captain and set up a new fundraising team Giving Page here.

I'm joining an existing fundraising team Join an existing fundraising team and you will be asked to set up a personal Giving Page. Any sponsorship you collect on your Giving Page will go to a collective total on your Team's Giving Page. Your Team Captain will be in charge of your Team's Giving Page but you can track your Team's progress and invite other Team Members to join via your own Giving Page.

To join other people who you know are fundraising, search for an existing fundraising team here, by entering the Team or Team Captain's name.
